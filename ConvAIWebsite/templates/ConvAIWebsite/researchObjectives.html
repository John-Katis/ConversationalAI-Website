Research Objectives

What constitutes argumentation quality in natural language and how can it be operationalized?
How to develop an argumentative writing support system for improving students’ argumentation skills?

Objective A

Create a rich semantic representation for fine-grained argumentation annotations capturing logical, rhetorical and dialectical quality dimensions.

Objective B

Create a large-scale, reusable argumentation test collection annotated for argumentation discourse structures and multidimensional quality features.

Objective C

Build an Argumentation Mining (AM) algorithm to automatically assess the quality of students’ reasoning in debates on controversial political and social topics.

How to develop a dialogue-based argumentation learning system that is able to debate with students on a controversial topic?
How to develop a dialogue-based argumentation learning system that is able to give interactive feedback about the quality of students’ argumentation?

Objective D

Build an argumentative dialogue agent for debating with students on controversial social and political issues with the objective of playfully improving their arguing skills.

Objective E

Create a dialogue-based approach for providing students with interactive feedback on their lines of argumentation.

Objective F

Create an analytical summary of the user’s arguing skills.

The expected outcomes of the project are twofold and relevant to both Science and practice. Those are the following:

Relevance for Science

  • Rich annotation scheme – captures multiple dimensions of argumentation quality
  • Large scale, annotated and reusable for similar purposes corpus
  • Novel approach for automatic quality assessment of argumentation
  • Novel dialogue based system to debate on controversial topics

    • Relevance for Practice

  • Help the transformation process towards more skill-based education following the OECD Learning Framework 2030
  • Support continuous learning of companies’ employees through argumentation learning support tools
  • Project outcomes and implemented prototypes can be leveraged in an Innosuisse project to ensure a broader practical application for companies’ continuous education
